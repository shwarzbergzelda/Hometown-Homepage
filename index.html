Visit Crown Heights

Enjoy culture and parks in this vibrant neighborhood in Brooklyn, New York.

Top three activities to do in Crown Heights

Go for a stroll in the park

Prospect Park is a great place to get out into nature!

Attend a concert or basketball game

Barclays Center is the perfect place to catch a basketball game or attend a concert

Visit a historic museum

The Brooklyn Museum is an ideal destination for those interested in history and art
